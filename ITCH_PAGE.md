## Description (English)

**Cube Survivor** is a fast, readable survivors-like set in a corrupted digital
arena. You auto-fire at endless swarms — your only jobs are to **move, dash, and
choose upgrades**. Every color means something: **you're blue, danger is red,
bosses are purple, rewards are gold.** One glance tells you where to be.

### Features
- ⚔️ **Auto-shooter combat** — dodge the swarm, let your weapons do the work.
- 🎲 **Blessings** — level up and draft upgrade cards with rarities and rerolls.
- 🏆 **Meta-progression** — earn **Cinders**, buy permanent upgrades, unlock new
  survivors, chase 12 achievements, and raise the difficulty with Ascension tiers.
- 🌍 **5 distinct stages & 6 bosses** — from the Verdant Grid to The Core, each
  with its own look, enemies, and telegraphed boss attacks.
- ⚡ **Runs great in the browser** — smooth even in the worst swarms.
- 🔊 **Full settings** — volume, screen shake, damage numbers, tutorial tips,
  fullscreen, and English / العربية.

### Controls
- **Move:** WASD / Arrow keys
- **Dash:** Space
- **Menus:** W/S select, A/D adjust, Enter confirm, `[U]` shop, `[T]` Ascension

Free to play in your browser. Grab the gold gems, dodge the red, and see how deep
into the arena you can push.
