# Cube Survivor

A fast, pixel-art roguelike survivor made in **Godot 4.7**. Survive five themed stages, face a boss every minute, and defeat the twice-reborn **Ash King** at the end.

## ▶️ Play in your browser
**https://darklawh.github.io/cube-survivor/**

(Also on itch.io: https://darklawh.itch.io/cube-survivor)

## Controls
- **WASD / Arrows** — move
- **Shift / Space** — dash (brief invulnerability)
- Shooting is **automatic** — it targets the nearest enemy
- **P** — pause

## Features
- 5 stages with unique ground, hazards and traps (Meadow, Desert, Glacier, Volcano, The Void)
- 6 bosses with distinct attack patterns + a final boss that resurrects
- 20 blessings and 3 weapon evolutions
- 4 playable survivors
- Everything drawn and scored **procedurally in code** — no external assets

## Run from source
1. Open the project folder in Godot 4.7
2. Press F5
